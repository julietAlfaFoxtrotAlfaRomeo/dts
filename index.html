Our Core Expertise

DAKARA, an Indonesian trading company, focuses on both international and domestic markets, with a primary focus on the Middle East. We trade in snacks, charcoal, briquettes, and aluminum (including scrap). By utilizing a third-party warehousing network strategically located, we ensure timely services to our customers. With our headquarters in Jakarta and subsidiaries across Java, we specialize in primary raw materials, semi-finished products, semi-fabricators, and downstream industries, establishing ourselves as a globally recognized leader in the industry.

Dried Cassava

Cassava, a resilient tropical plant, is vital for global food security as a staple crop. Various names like yucca, or mandioca, it embodies adaptability and nutritional value. Our company specializes in processing cassava into dried form, maintaining high quality through partnerships with Indonesian farmers. This ensures a steady supply of 25,000 - 50,000 monthly.

Charcoal

Dakara Tunas Sentosa is a leading trader of premium coconut shell charcoal for Hookah and BBQ. Our commitment to innovation drives us to serve international markets, catering to the specific quality demands of regions like Asia, the Middle East, America, and Europe. We play a vital role in boosting the Indonesian economy through our operations in the briquette industry.

ALUMINIUM

We specialize in manufacturing and marketing raw material products for the Construction & Automobile Industry, particularly extrusion billets. Using Airslip Technology & Continuous Homogenization furnaces, we ensure high quality. Our billets are versatile, capable of being extruded into various profiles for soft to high strength applications.
